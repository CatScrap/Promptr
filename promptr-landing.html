Last Updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

CatScrap ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Promptr mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Promptr, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Apple Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Unique Apple User ID (required)
  • Email address (if you choose to share it)
  • Full name (if you choose to share it)

2.2 Prompt Creation Data

We store:

  • Prompts you create and save
  • Prompt titles, descriptions, tags, and categories
  • Prompt creation and modification timestamps
  • Version history of your prompts
  • Prompts you share publicly or with the community

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (features used, errors encountered)
  • Firebase Analytics data (crashes, performance metrics)
  • Browsing activity within the community prompt library

2.4 Subscription & Payment Data

If you subscribe to Premium or make in-app purchases:

  • Subscription status and expiration date
  • Credit balance for premium features
  • Purchase history (processed by Apple, not stored by us)

3. Why We Collect This Data

3.1 Legal Accountability

Important: We require authentication because users create and share content publicly. Linking prompts to verified Apple IDs protects our service and users from:

  • Spam and low-quality content
  • Harmful or offensive material
  • Copyright infringement
  • Impersonation and fraud

3.2 Service Functionality

  • Sync your prompts across devices
  • Maintain your subscription and credit balance
  • Enable community sharing and discovery features
  • Provide personalized recommendations

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers:

  • Location: United States (us-central1)
  • Encryption: At rest and in transit
  • Retention: Until you delete your account

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase (cloud hosting)
  • Apple App Store (payment processing)
  • When required by law or court order
  • Publicly shared prompts are visible to other users

Contact us to request a copy of your data.

6.3 Export Your Prompts

Use the Export feature to save your prompts before deleting your account.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted storage (Firebase encryption)
  • Authentication-required API access
  • Regular security audits
